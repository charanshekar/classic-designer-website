Our History

In 1986, then budding and aspiring designer

- J Lakshminarayan
founded Classic Designer in Bangalore to contribute his artistic ideas of design to craft classy outfits for the tailored gentleman. The clothing line quickly established itself as a go to Store for quality and premium design suiting, formal wear and wedding apparel. The handy craftmanship and out of the box designs have only become that much better over time, always providing Fashion that is a Class apart.
